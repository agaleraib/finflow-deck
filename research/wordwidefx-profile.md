## Identity
- **Trade name:** Wordwide FX — Financial Translation Services
- **Tagline:** "Translation & Localisation for Trading, Finance and Crypto"
- **Secondary:** "Much more than your translation provider"
- **Founded:** 2010-2011, Barcelona, Spain
- **Founders:** Alex Galera and Isabel Galera
- **Website:** https://wordwidefx.com

## Origin Story
Alex and Isabel identified a gap in the market — quality financial content in non-English languages was consistently poor. FX companies were neglecting high-quality translation as part of their marketing strategies. They created a specialist provider focused exclusively on financial markets.

## Services
1. **Website translation** — Trading platforms, exchanges, wallets, Web3 apps
2. **Market news & analyses** — Financial reports, TA/FA, crypto research, token insights
3. **Marketing content** — Product pages, blogs, learning hubs, whitepapers
4. **Legal documents** — Compliance materials, agreements, T&Cs, disclosures, risk notices
5. **Educational material** — User guides, help centres, knowledge bases, FAQs
6. **Trading software localization** — Client portals, dashboards, apps

## Industries
Forex brokers, cryptocurrency exchanges, proprietary trading firms, CFD providers, stock platforms, banking — both regulated and non-regulated financial markets.

## Key Differentiators
- **100% human translation** (historically — no machine translation)
- Financial/trading specialists, not generalists
- Translators with expertise "comparable to market analysts"
- Fast turnaround for urgent projects
- Scalable workflows for high-volume content
- Dedicated project managers per client
